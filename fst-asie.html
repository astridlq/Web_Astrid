Food Sense Tour Asie

Showing solutions, telling a feel-good story… this may be the best way to solve the ecological crises that our countries are going through.
The aim of the Food Sense Tour is to document the activities of entrepreneurs chosen upstream for their contribution to the sustainable food product cycle: production, distribution, consumption and recycling. Those concrete and positive actions in the agriculture field are a first step to figure out what could be tomorrow's world…

This website aimed at promoting a Food Sense Tour around Asia...

Graphic Design

This final project was part of a Graphic Design course I follow during my Master's degree at Hong-Kong University of Science and Technology (2017). The aim was to promote one project of our choice and to create graphic components thanks to Photoshop, Illustrator...

I used the WIX platform to showcase a website of my project. The idea was to have a landing page explaining the whole concept of a Food Sense Tour, the itinerary I would do across Asia and a short intro of myself.
Users could then find out more information on different sections: About, Project, Portraits, Sponsors.

I used green colors for the main theme as it relates to the environment, trees and plants.
For the logo, I decided to design it in a round shape to give the idea of a “tour”. I also wanted to create a font for the capital letter “A” that would remind the user of chopsticks and rice – Asia it is!
