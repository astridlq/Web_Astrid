Recruit.me

Employee Retention rates at many companies is low (and dropping). According to a study led by Deloitte in 2018, it's less than 2 years at many big tech companies and the cost of losing one employee can range from tens of thousands of dollars, to 1.5-2 times their annual salary.
So how come that big companies - which have so many job opportunities - fail at retaining talents in house?

Imagine an platform helping managers to find talents easily within their organisation and empowering employees to discover new job opportunities. And all this based on skills...

This platform is

  • For employees of corporate international companies,
  • Who need to move on to their next challenge / hire a new employee,
  • And want to find a perfect match (job / employee) within the company.
  • They choose Recruit.me because it enables them to grow within the same organization or to find talents already familiar with the company culture… (unlike LinkedIn!)

User Journey

  • Anna goes on the web application.
  • She logs in.
  • Navigates to the manager portal.
  • Searches for candidates with specific skills.
  • Browses the matching candidates.
  • Sees a potential candidate.
  • Views the profile.
  • Messages the candidate.
